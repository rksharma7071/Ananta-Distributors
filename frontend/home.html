This one and the old one has a major design flaw. Once you run the wire throughout the car if one light goes bad you have to remove all the wiring for that line. Why not design it where we can easily remove the light without removing the whole wiring line by creating a connector between the light and the wire we have to run through the car. I bought these to install on my MDX. I initially plan to use only six of the lights. But after some tinkering with them, I realized it four. one in each wheel was enough light for the fact that I was gone for. I tapped them into my headlight electrical wiring so that they turn on whenever the headlights are on. I debated tapping them into my fog lights so that I had control of them from my dash. Ultimately I went with the headlight. I didn't run the power button into the cab of the car since they are just power to the headlights and come on automatically when the headlights are on. I didn't mess with the colors or do the app. I just wanted pure white light. It's a very good quality lights and there's enough cable with them to do some cool stuff with them if you want to light up the underneath of your car. For my application they turned out great I definitely recommend them.
